Information that We Collect and How We Use It.

Notice: your Hover Camera product will automatically record the following informations:

  • Collect the way you use the app and the features you use in order to optimize our app and the interaction experience.
  • Related facial information will be collected and upload to server when you use the face recognition feature in order to optimize algorithm and improve the accuracy of face recognition.
  • If the product fails to track you when you use the face-tracking or body-tracking mode, the pictures before the failure will be upload to the server in order to optimize the robustness of auto tracking;
  • Hover Camera will automatically intercept the current screen shot and save the data in the system in the process of using the product; but these images will not be uploaded to the server, they are only used in the after-sale process to find problems or determining the responsibility.
  • When users use the App to provide shared information to the third party through our services, or if they store some information when using our services, these kinds of information will be collected.
  • Shared information provided by other third parties when they use our services will be collected.

Each time you use the App, we need to determine your location via the geolocation (GPS) feature of your Smart phone or other mobile device to allow the App to determine which Wi-Fi channel or bandwidth is appropriate for controlling your Hover Camera product in your then-current location. The SDK we used in the app may collect certain information automatically including, but without limitation, the type of mobile device you use, your mobile device’s unique device ID, the IP address of your mobile device, your mobile operating system, your preferred language, the type of mobile Internet browsers you use, and information about the way you use the App. This information will only be used for the development and optimization of product features and will not be disclosed or disclosed in any form without the authorization of the user.

By using the App, whether as a registered user or otherwise, you acknowledge and agree that you are giving us your consent to track your activities and your use of the App through the technologies described above, as well as similar technologies developed in the future.

If you choose to register with us when downloading the App, we will obtain whatever information you provide to us (some of which is required to establish your registration and is marked with an asterisk (*), and other information, even if a field is made available for completion, is entirely voluntary). Please note that, currently, registration with us is completely optional and is not required in order to use the App (but clicking the “I Agree” button at the end of the Terms of Use to signify your acceptance of the Terms of Use and this Privacy Policy is required in order to use the App). It is possible that you may not be able to use some of the features offered by the App, now or in the future, unless you register with us. The App does not seek to access your phone book or contact lists, nor to identify any other mobile applications that you may have downloaded onto the Smart phone or other device onto which you have downloaded and are using the App.

If, in the future, we begin to allow users to post content to the Zero Zero website (so-called “user generated content,” or “UGC”) through the App, individuals who want to use the App for such a purpose or to communicate with us will be required to register with us and create accounts, which will entail setting up account passwords and log-in credentials.
